# Gridiron Front Office — One Pager (v1.1)

**Build the roster. Beat the clock. Survive the league.**

## Elevator Pitch
**Gridiron Front Office** is a hardcore football franchise simulation built entirely from the **front-office point of view**. You run the organization—roster construction, drafting, coaching direction, budgets, facilities, and owner expectations—while the league evolves around you with realistic talent cycles designed to prevent permanent dominance.

Built for franchise-sim and strategy-management players, it combines **data-forward decision-making**, an **elite modern UI**, and **real-time key moments** (Draft, Free Agency, Trade Deadline) that capture the pressure of running a team when the clock doesn't stop.

## Vision (Design Pillars)
- **Front Office First**: you don't win with stick skills—you win with decisions.
- **Real-Time Pressure Where It Matters**: the Draft, Free Agency, and the Trade Deadline unfold on the clock to reward preparation and decisive action.
- **Dynasties Are Temporary**: long-term challenge is protected by natural roster cycles and capable AI franchises.
- **Data Without Pain**: surface the right information at the right time—fast, readable, and actionable.
- **Simple to Operate, Deep to Master**: clean UI with meaningful depth, not spreadsheet fatigue.

## The Hook
A true front-office sim where **timing is a mechanic**: the **Draft, Free Agency, and Trade Deadline** play out in real time (with at most limited pause), forcing you to commit, adapt, and live with consequences—while **Dynasty Decay** keeps the league competitive and believable over many seasons.

## What You Do (Player Role)
You are the GM / President of Football Operations. You:
- Build and maintain a competitive roster under realistic cap and budget constraints
- Navigate drafting and free agency with timing pressure and imperfect information
- Set coaching direction and organizational priorities that shape team identity
- Balance win-now pushes vs long-term sustainability under an owner with a distinct personality

## Core Gameplay Loop (High Level)
- **Week-to-week cadence** follows realistic football timelines: manage roster, finances, and the organization, then simulate games and advance the season.
- **Real-time event windows** create high-stakes decision points:
  - **Draft**: clock-driven picks and trade decisions
  - **Free Agency**: a moving market with timed opportunities
  - **Trade Deadline**: rapid negotiation and execution before the window closes

## Key Features
- **Realistic Franchise Management**: cap space, budgets, owner patience, draft capital, facilities, and long-term planning.
- **Real-Time Draft / Free Agency / Trade Deadline**: pressure-driven systems where preparation matters and hesitation costs you.
- **Player Archetypes by Position**: multiple archetypes per position (generally ~4) to support distinct roster-building approaches.
- **Owner Personalities**: different ownership styles that shift priorities and pressure:
  - **Aggressive**
  - **Conservative**
  - **Money-Focused**
  - **Legacy-Driven**
- **Modern UI / Great UX**: a clean, data-driven interface designed to keep decisions fast and clear.

## Dynasty Decay (Long-Term Challenge)
Gridiron Front Office is built around the belief that dominance should not be permanent. Every franchise—human and AI—faces **natural decay pressure** over time (aging curves, league competition, and evolving talent distribution), ensuring the league stays challenging and believable across many seasons.

## Setting
**Inspired by realism**: roster sizes, cap dynamics, and season structure are grounded in real-world football constraints while remaining its own simulation experience.
